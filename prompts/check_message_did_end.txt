Analyze the message from the user and determine if they have finished speaking, no matter how short or long the message is.

Indications of incomplete messages can be filler words ("like", etc.)

- Incomplete sentences (e.g. mid-sentence, mid-thought) are not complete messages.
- Think about what a complete sentence looks like in a turn-based conversation, does the text make sense as a complete sentence, statement, question, thought, etc?
